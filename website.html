Each day, we put on clothes that do more than just cover up bodies. We choose clothes that represent our personalities, our moods, the times we live in, our ambitions and our desires.

Mused by her surroundings and influenced by her piercing capacity of depicuing natural colors, shapes and forms, Hoda Ojail translated her gift into her fashion creativity

Hoda was born in Beirut, Lebanon in 1973. She started her trip with fashion at the most important Lebanese fashion houses and embraced different fashion tastes by the most important Lebanese fashion designers like Zuhair Mourad, George Hoberka and Jean Fares.

Married with 3 children, Hoda thrives with their support and encouragement. She says "my boys are my muse and my foundation". Distinguished herself by being innovative at the embroidery world and by using stones and crystals to turn each innovation into a masterpiece that each Arabian lady, looking for glory, deserves. She decided to open her atelier at Cairo, Egypt after she got inspired by its inexhaustible treasures of civilization...
